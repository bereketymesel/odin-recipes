Lasagna

Description

This classic lasagna is rich, cheesy, and full of flavor. It's perfect for a family dinner or to impress your guests.

Layered with noodles, meat sauce, and creamy ricotta, it’s a hearty Italian favorite you'll love.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can tomato sauce
  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook ground beef, onion, and garlic in a pan until browned.
  3. Stir in tomato sauce and simmer for 15 minutes.
  4. Boil lasagna noodles until al dente, then drain.
  5. Spread sauce in baking dish, layer noodles, ricotta, mozzarella, and repeat.
  6. Top with Parmesan and bake for 25-30 minutes.
  7. Let it cool for 10 minutes before serving.
