Pizza

Description

A classic homemade pizza with a crispy crust, tangy tomato sauce, and melty cheese.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Pepperoni slices
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out dough on a floured surface.
  3. Spread tomato sauce evenly over dough.
  4. Top with cheese and pepperoni.
  5. Bake for 12-15 minutes until crust is golden.
